WHAT IF I'M NOT HOME WHEN YOU PICKUP AND DELIVERY?

No problem. When you sign up, we take note of a pre-designated location where you can leave your laundry that is safe from the elements and accessible to us. Some customers have us leave their laundry at their back door or in an apartment foyer if they are not home.

IS THERE ANY WAY THAT SIMPLY CLEAN CAN REMIND ME TO LEAVE MY LAUNDRY OUT OF PICKUP?

Sure. We send all of our customers an email reminder the day before scheduled pickups.

CAN MY TOOMATE AND I SHARE AN ACCOUNT?

Sure, but for your convenience we highly recommend you each sign up for your own account . If you do decide to share an account, clothes will be washed and packed together so you will have to separate your laundry upon its return.
